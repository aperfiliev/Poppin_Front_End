ARIANNA HUFFINGTON
Chair, President and Editor-In-Chief
of The Huffington Post
”Working in media — or
any industry, really —
comes with a built-in challenge: it’s entirely possible to spend an entire day without ever taking your eyes off a screen.

This way of working comes with real costs to our creativity, productivity, and ability to truly engage with ideas and each other. That’s why we’re giving all HuffPost employees notebooks and encouraging device-free meetings. The benefits of putting down our devices and opting instead for pen and paper aren’t just common sense, they’re backed by science: studies show writing by hand actually engages more areas of the brain, improving our ability to focus and remember what we’re hearing.

We’re delighted to be partnering with Poppin, a maker of beautiful, completely-non-digital notebooks, whose motto ‘work happy’ is totally in line with our HuffPost ethos.”
